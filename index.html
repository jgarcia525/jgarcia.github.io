My Bio

If you look closely, my tie has flamingos.

I am a third-year Computer Science major at the University of Texas at Austin with a minor in Entrepreneurship. I am currently a part-time intern at a startup called Mindsight, where I do front-end web development using Vue.js and Bulma. I am incredibly involved in the Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE), and I am the Athletics Director for this year. Also, I am the President of the Div Day Committee which is a committee composed of members from the Hispanic Association of Computer Scientists (HACS) and the Association of Black Computer Scientists (ABCS). To elaborate on Div Day, it is a conference hosted at UT Austin focused on promoting diversity and inclusion in tech and STEM.
